In cryptography large integers are fundamental, so you should start with the Base10Number.cpp, Integer.cpp, and IntegerMath.cpp files, in that order, first.

Look at MainApp.cpp to see what is current. It has the mainLoop() function and you can follow it from there. The main() function in main.cpp just creates and runs the MainApp.cpp object.
